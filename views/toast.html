Toast

Toasts are primarily used for system messaging.
Toasts can't contain actions and can't be swiped off screen. They should contain a single line of text directly related to the operation performed. They can't contain icons.
Send

For js:

app.controller('AppCtrl', ['$scope', '$mdToast', function($scope, $mdToast){
    $scope.openToast = function($event) {
        $mdToast.show($mdToast.simple().content('Message sent'));
};

For MD:

<div layout="row" layout-align="left">
    <md-button md-no-ink class="md-raised" ng-click="openToast()">Send</md-button>
</div>
